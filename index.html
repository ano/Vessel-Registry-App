STEP 2: PAY

We check and verify that your vessel and company has a good compliance history. If so we will issue you an invoice for payment. You will be required to pay this and then upload Proof of Payment (i.e. a Copy of the Telegraphic Transfer).

UPLOAD PROOF OF PAYMENT SCHEDULE OF FEES

STEP 3: GOODSTANDING

Once we confirmed your payment has been deposited, we will put you onto our vessel registry of goodstanding.

VESSEL GOODSTANDING

STEP 4: ISSUE

We issue you a digital certificate of goodstanding. If your vessel is new we issue you a unique identifier for your vessel. This is known as your FFA Vessel ID or FFA VID for short. You will need to provide this to FFA member countries when applying for fishing permits / licenses.
